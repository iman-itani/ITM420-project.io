Use-Case Name:
Remove doctor
 Use Case Type
Business Requirements þ
Use-Case ID:
Priority:
Source:
Interview 2
Primary System Actor:
Administrator
Primary Business Actor:
Description:
This use case involves accessing the system's management interface, selecting the doctor to be removed from the list of available doctors, confirming the removal, and notifying relevant parties within the organization of the removal, while the system updates the list of doctors to reflect the removal.
Precondition:
The administrator must have a valid login credential to access the system and must be authorized to remove a doctor.
Trigger:
A doctor will no longer provide services at the medical center.
Typical Course Of Events:
Actor
System response

 
 Step 1: the administrator accesses the system's management interface and navigates to the section for removing doctors.
Step 3: The administrator selects the doctor to be removed from the list.
Step 5: The administrator confirms the removal of the doctor.
Step 8: The administrator notifies relevant parties within the organization, such as the medical staff, of the removal of the doctor.
 Step 2: The interface presents a list of all available doctors using EXtract Data use case.
Step 4: The system prompts the administrator to confirm the removal of the doctor.
Step 6: The system removes the doctor from the list of available doctors and displays a confirmation message.
Step 7: The system updates the list of doctors to reflect the removal of the doctor.
Alternate Courses:
 Alt to step 5: The administrator cancels the removal request.
  • The system cancels the removal request and returns to the previous screen.
Conclusion:
Upon successful completion of the use-case, the selected doctor is removed from the system, and patients cannot book appointments with them anymore.
Postcondition:
The selected doctor record is removed from the system, and cashier cannot add them in transactions record.
Business Rules:
 The system must have a mechanism to authenticate the user before removing a doctor.
Only authorized personnel should be able to remove a doctor.
Implementation Constraints and Specifications:
The system must have a backup and recovery mechanism in case of data loss or system failure.
Assumptions:
The administrator selects the correct doctor to be removed.
