Use-Case Name:

Daily reports generation
Use Case Type
Business Requirements ��
Use-Case ID:

 
Priority:

 
Source:
Interview 2
 
Primary System Actor:
Cashier
Primary Business Actor:
 
Description:
This use-case allows the cashier to generate daily reports on the system's operations. The daily reports can include information such as the number of patients who received services on a particular day, the number of services provided by a specific doctor, and other relevant information.
Precondition:
The cashier must have the necessary permissions and access to the system's management interface.
The system must have recorded daily transactional data.
Trigger:
Daily reports are generated based on doctor’s requests to get a summary of their performance in a certain day.
Typical Course Of Events:
Actor
System response

 
Step 1: The cashier logs in to the system and navigates to the "Generate Daily Reports" option.
Step 3: The cashier sets the date of the current day with the required or intended information and submits it.
Step 6: the cashier View the report and send it to the concerned personnel accordingly.
Step 2: The system displays a list of filters and checkboxes to filter out the information that needs to be included in the report on a specific date.
Step 4: Use case Extract Data
Step 5: The system generates the desired daily report based on selections.
Alternate Courses:
None
Conclusion:

The cashier is able to generate and review daily reports on the system's operations to gain insights about daily operations.
Postcondition:
The cashier has accessed and reviewed the desired daily reports.
Business Rules:
The cashier must have the necessary authorization to access and generate daily reports
Assumptions:
Daily transactional data is being accurately and timely recorded in the system
