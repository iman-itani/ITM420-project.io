Use-Case Name:
Issue receipt voucher
 Use Case Type
Business Requirements þ

Source:
Interview 2
Primary System Actor:
Accountant
Primary Business Actor:
Other Participating Actors:
Other Interested Stakeholders:
Donor
Description:
This use case involves the process of generating a receipt and voucher for transactions covered by a donor. When a donation is made by a donor, the accountant generates a receipt to acknowledge the donation and also generates a voucher to provide the donor with proof of the donation.
Precondition:
The medical center has items available in the stock to sell.
Trigger:
A donation is made by a donor
Typical Course of Events:
Actor
System response

 
 Step 1: The accountant receives information about the donation made by the donor.
Step 3: The accountant enters the information into the system.
Step 5: The accountant provides the receipt and voucher to the donor.
 Step 2: The system displays the receipt voucher template that needs to be filled.
Step 4: The system generates a voucher for the donation.
Step 6: The system saves a copy of the voucher in the database.
Alternate Courses:
Alternate step 5: If the donor disputes the details on the voucher, the accountant investigates the issue and resolves it with the donor.
Conclusion:
The donor receives a receipt and voucher for the donation made to the medical center.
Postcondition:
The receipt and voucher have been provided to the donor.
Business Rules:
 The receipt and voucher must accurately reflect the donation made by the donor.
The receipt and voucher must be generated in a timely manner after the donation has been received
Implementation Constraints and Specifications:
 The system must have the capability to generate a receipt and voucher.
The receipt and voucher must be formatted appropriately and include all required information.
Assumptions:
None
