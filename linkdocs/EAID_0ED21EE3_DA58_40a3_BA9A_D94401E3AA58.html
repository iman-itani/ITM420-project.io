Use-Case Name:
Adjust a service
Use Case Type
Business Requirements þ
Use-Case ID:
Priority:
Source:
Interview 2
Primary System Actor:
Administrator
Primary Business Actor:
Other Participating Actors:
Description:
Change the price of a service in the system, so that patients are charged the updated amount for that service
Precondition:
The administrator must have a valid login credential to access the system and must be authorized to change service prices.
Trigger:
A change of price occurs due to the change in the market or other various factors
Typical Course Of Events:
Actor
System response

 
 Step 1: The administrator logs in to the system using valid login credentials and selects the option to modify a service.
Step 3: The administrator updates the price(s) of the service (s) and confirms the updates.
 Step 2: The system displays a list of all available services in the system with their current prices using Extract Data use case.
Step 4: The system saves the updated service.
Step 5: The system notifies the concerned users about the changes of prices that occurred.
Alternate Courses:
 Alternate to step 3:  The administrator cancels the price change request.
  • The system cancels the price change request and returns to the previous screen.
Conclusion:
Upon successful completion of the use-case, the price of the selected service is updated, and patients are charged the updated amount for that service.
Postcondition:
The price of the selected service is updated, and patients are charged the updated amount for that service.
Business Rules:
Only authorized personnel should be able to change service prices.
Implementation Constraints and Specifications:
The system must have a backup and recovery mechanism in case of data loss or system failure.
Assumptions:
The administrator enters the correct updated price for the service.
