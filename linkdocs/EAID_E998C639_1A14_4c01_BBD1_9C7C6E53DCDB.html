Use-Case Name:
Add doctor
 Use Case Type

Use-Case ID:
Priority:
Source:
Interview 2
Primary System Actor:
Administrator
Primary Business Actor:
-
Description:
This use case involve accessing the system's management interface, filling out a form with the necessary information for the new doctor, submitting the request, and notifying relevant parties within the organization of the new doctor's availability, while the system creates a new doctor record and integrates it into the system.
Precondition:
 The administrator must have a valid login credential to access the system and must be authorized to add a new doctor.
The added doctor should have signed a contract with the medical center.
Trigger:
The company sign a contract with a new doctor to start providing services at the medical center.
Typical Course Of Events:
Actor
System response

 
 Step 1: the administrator accesses the system's management interface and navigates to the section for adding new doctors.
Step 3: The administrator fills out the form with the necessary information and submits it.
Step 5: The administrator confirms the changes made.
Step 7: The administrator notifies the relevant parties within the organization, such as the medical staff, of the new doctor's availability.
Step 8: The administrator can now view the new doctor in the system's directory and make any necessary adjustments to their profile or settings.
 Step 2: The interface presents a form for entering the details of the new doctor, including their name, contact information, medical specialties, and any other relevant information.
Step 4: The system processes the request and verifies that all required fields have been completed correctly.
Step 6: Once the request is successfully submitted and validated, the system creates a new doctor record and adds it to the list of available doctors.
Alternate Courses:
 Alternate to step 4:  The data entered by the administrator is invalid.
    • The system displays an error message indicating that the data entered is invalid.
    • The administrator re-enters the data.
    • The use-case continues from step 3.
Alternate to step 5: The administrator cancels the doctor addition request.
    • The system cancels the addition request and returns to the previous screen.
Conclusion:
Upon successful completion of the use-case, a new doctor is added to the system with all necessary details, and patients can receive treatment from them.
Postcondition:
A new doctor record is created in the system with all necessary details and can be selected by the cashier while recording transactions.
Business Rules:
 The system must have a mechanism to authenticate the user before adding a new doctor.
Only authorized personnel should be able to add a new doctor.
 
Implementation Constraints and Specifications:
The system must have a backup and recovery mechanism in case of data loss or system failure.
Assumptions:
The details entered by the administrator are accurate and complete.
